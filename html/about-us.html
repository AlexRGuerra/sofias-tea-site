About Sofia's Tea

In the municipality of Mississauga, Ontario, two sisters, Sofia and Tea Falzata, established a bakery venture. Their establishment, known as "Sofia's Tea," was founded upon a shared enthusiasm for the culinary arts, cultivated in their mother's kitchen. Committed to utilizing locally sourced ingredients, the sisters curated a menu of meticulously crafted pastries, cakes, and confections which we now sell with delight. The now franchise began as a home business and we are now looking to expand into franchises across the Mississauga area!

Baking can be a wasteful process, but we make the most out of it. Rather than disposing of the left-over food we have we make an effort to donate it to various communities in the area. Help us change the baking industry by helping those in need.
