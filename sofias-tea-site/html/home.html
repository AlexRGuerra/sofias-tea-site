Our History

How and Why we Started

In the municipality of Mississauga, Ontario, two sisters, Sofia and Tea Falzata, established a bakery venture. Their establishment, known as "Sofia's Tea," was founded upon a shared enthusiasm for the culinary arts, cultivated in their mother's kitchen. Committed to utilizing locally sourced ingredients, the sisters curated a menu of meticulously crafted pastries, cakes, and confections which we now sell with delight.

Pastries

We feature over 20 different kinds of pastries all of which are beloved by our costumers for their flaky, buttery, and often sweet or savory goodness. Each type has its own unique preparation method, cultural significance, and flavor profile, making pastries a beloved and versatile category of baked goods worldwide.

Cookies

Indulge in the sheer delight of our exquisite cookies, each one a masterpiece of flavor and texture. Crafted with the utmost care, our cookies boast a harmonious blend of premium ingredients, ensuring every bite is a symphony of rich, buttery goodness. From the velvety chocolate chips to the perfectly toasted nuts, every component is meticulously chosen for its quality.
